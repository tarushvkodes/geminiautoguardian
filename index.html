About Us

Welcome to Auto Guardian, your reliable roadside assistance partner in Hyderabad. Founded by the Kosgi Family and Raja Chandra Gajula, we are dedicated to ensuring that no driver is ever stranded on the roads of Hyderabad. Our mission is to provide prompt, efficient, and trustworthy roadside assistance services, giving you peace of mind whenever you are behind the wheel.

We understand the stress and inconvenience of vehicle breakdowns. That's why Auto Guardian is committed to being "Always On Guard for You," offering a comprehensive suite of services designed to get you back on your journey as quickly and safely as possible. Trust us to be your guardian on the road.

Our Services

Roadside Assistance

Towing Services
